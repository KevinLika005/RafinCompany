    Project delivery

    Services structured for disciplined execution

    Rafin combines planning, field coordination, technical oversight, and delivery control to support complex infrastructure and construction work from concept to completion.

    Integrated capabilities

    How we support each stage of delivery

    Our services are aligned around practical delivery needs: front-end definition, coordinated execution, quality control, and dependable handover.

    01

    Planning and preparation

    Pre-construction Services

    We define scope, technical requirements, sequencing, and delivery risks before site execution starts, helping projects move into construction with stronger control and fewer surprises.

    • Initial technical review and coordination of design intent.
    • Preliminary cost, resources, schedule, and site-readiness planning.
    • Identification of execution risks, interfaces, and critical approvals.
    02

    Delivery coordination

    General Contracting

    We coordinate specialist teams, procurement interfaces, and day-to-day delivery obligations so projects stay aligned with the required quality, timeframe, and operational priorities.

    • Coordination of subcontractors, suppliers, and site interfaces.
    • Clear execution responsibilities, progress tracking, and issue management.
    • Quality-focused supervision from mobilization through handover.
    03

    Site execution

    Construction Services

    We execute civil and infrastructure works with structured site operations, technical discipline, and close attention to workmanship, safety, and field coordination.

    • Organized field operations for civil, road, utility, and related works.
    • Execution monitoring focused on quality, productivity, and sequence control.
    • Practical coordination between crews, machinery, and supporting trades.
    04

    Control and reporting

    Construction Management

    We manage project execution through reporting, coordination, schedule follow-up, and decision support so stakeholders have a clear view of progress, constraints, and priorities.

    • Progress planning, schedule follow-up, and delivery reporting.
    • Coordination of technical decisions, approvals, and work interfaces.
    • Monitoring of cost, quality, and time-sensitive milestones.
    05

    Digital coordination

    Building Information

    We use digital information and model-based coordination where needed to improve clarity, reduce clashes, and support better technical communication across the project team.

    • Support for project visualization, model review, and technical coordination.
    • Clearer communication of dimensions, systems, and construction intent.
    • Better coordination between design information and field execution.
    06

    Integrated approach

    Design-Build

    For suitable project types, we support integrated design-build delivery to simplify interfaces, improve decision speed, and align project development with execution requirements.

    • Closer connection between design development and construction planning.
    • Faster coordination across technical, cost, and scheduling decisions.
    • A practical route for projects that benefit from unified delivery responsibility.
